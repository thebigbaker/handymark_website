About

I am the sole owner and operator of HandyMark Sunnyside. I will be there from start to finish. I won’t give you an estimate and send a stranger to your home. I will complete your project in an expert and timely fashion. The job won’t be considered done until you are fully satisfied!

I am relatively new to the Denver area. I was stationed at Fort Carson in Colorado Springs as a young soldier and wanted to get back here as soon as I could. I love the Colorado outdoor lifestyle and enjoyed building a teardrop trailer to take advantage of our beautiful backyard. Let me ease the stress of chores so you can get out and enjoy all Colorado has to offer.

I enjoy working with my hands and doing home improvement projects. After retiring from the Army flying helicopters, being a handyman is the perfect fit. I enjoy meeting new people and helping with their projects.

I am punctual and will arrive on time, you can count on it. I hear it all the time about contractors not showing up at all or late without any communication. You WILL NOT have that issue with me.
